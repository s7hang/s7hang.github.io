I am Shuo Zhang, currently at Renmin University of China.

My research interests include algorithmic game theory, mechanism design, information design, digital auctions, and algorithms. I am particularly interested in the design and analysis of mechanisms under informational constraints, as well as efficient algorithms for geometric and economic problems.

## Research Interests

- Algorithmic game theory
- Mechanism design and auction theory
- Information design and signaling
- Approximation algorithms
- Computational geometry, including Fréchet distance

## Publications

Recent publications include work accepted to EC, STOC, and WWW. For theory papers with alphabetical author ordering, I mark the author order with `(α-β)`.

- **Private Private Information in Second-Price Auction**. EC 2026, oral. `(α-β)`
- **Constant Approximation of Fréchet Distance in Strongly Subquadratic Time**. STOC 2025, oral; invited to Special Issue, top 10%. `(α-β)`
- **Competitive Information Design with Asymmetric Senders**. EC 2024, oral. `(α-β)`
- **On Truthful Item-Acquiring Mechanisms for Reward Maximization**. WWW 2024, poster.
